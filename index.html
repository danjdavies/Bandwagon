Welcome to Bandwagon

What is this all about then?

I don't like starting out from scratch every time and wanted some sort of base template to begin with. Inspired by a few people I know, I decided to write s suite of files that would be I guess a project starting point for anything really. The aim was to get others collaborating on it, hence the "jumping on the bandwagon" theme. I'm treating it as a playground for me to develop new skills, get better at what I do and work with other devs on what I hope some people might actually use.

Maybe I'll do a WordPress theme out of it as part of my Browser Led Design approach. The possibilities are endless.

Why have you done this that way? It's wrong! Are you an idiot n00b?

I stress, these are not finished files, they are not even tidy. I'm developing as I go. I'll keep returning to bits and changing and I'm sure others will join in and make it a collaborative masterpiece.

How can I get involved?

Twitter, email or Github is where you can find me. Ask away. I want to get people involved. I want to learn as well as teach.
